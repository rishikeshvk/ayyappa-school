Events

At Sree Ayyappa Education Centre (CBSE) Bangalore, academic excellence and co-curricular activities always go hand-in-hand. Keeping in view the personality development of every individual student, the school organizes varied events throughout the year.

Students of the primary and senior school are actively involved in inter-school competitions. Inter-school events are a conglomeration of numerous activities in various spheres such as art, humanities, science and so on.

Each of these events is the brainchild of our students and they are managed professionally.

The Inter-House activities include a whole gamut of literary and cultural activities like Elocution, Declamation, Debate, General Quiz, Calligraphy in Hindi and English, Indian Light Vocal , Painting, Drawing , Group Dance, fancy dress, Collage and many more.

The Inter-house events conducted at school level are open to students of all grades.


Co-Curricular Activities

Extra curricular activities are conducted in the school so that the student can nurture their inborn talents under the able guidance of professionals. At Sree Ayyappa Education Centre (CBSE), a student has a plethora of activities to choose from. The extra-curricular activities provide further avenues to enable the students to enhance their artistic skills.

Physical and Health Education, Art Education and Work Experience are compulsory and are dealt with effectively in the non-scholastic area.


Sports Activities

After a day of exercising their grey cells, students need to cool off with sports. Throwball, Volleyball, Foot ball, Cricket , track and field practices are held very often.

Sports not only build the students' concentration levels but also helps improve their team skills.

Inter - house matches are crowd pullers. Everyone gathers to support their house and urge them to win.

Sports on the whole adds spice to the celebration.
