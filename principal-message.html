Principal's Message


The future belongs to those who believe in the beauty of their dreams
Each and everyday ----
Each and every child
We have the power, the passion, the skills and the
knowledge to make the difference.

Education is an ongoing process in the life time of an individual, a process from being to becoming.

Child is a precious gift and nurturing him/her with love and care is a sense of responsibility.

Realization of an individual’s potential is one of the basic aims of education.

Each student should realize that he has the potential & education should help him to attain great heights.The contribution of school in building character and intellectual capacity of a child is immense.

We, SAEC (CBSE) give utmost priority to provide enhancing environment that promotes self discipline, positive attitude, self esteem, goal setting and inculcate values in them, so that righteousness in the heart is developed in young minds.

We impart quality education that is holistic in every aspect, which help the students to grow socially sensitive and responsible citizens who will soar to great heights in life.

With Warm Regards
Manimegalai
Principal SAEC (CBSE)
